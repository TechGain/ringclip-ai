Privacy Policy

RingClip AI — Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with TikTok, we receive your basic profile information (username and user ID) through TikTok's OAuth flow. This is used solely to post content on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account for content posting.
  • To publish AI-generated videos to your TikTok account at your request.

3. Data Storage

We do not store personal data beyond the active session. Authentication tokens are used temporarily and are not persisted after the session ends.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Content is posted directly to TikTok via their official API.

5. Third-Party Services

This Service integrates with:

  • TikTok — for content posting (subject to TikTok's Terms)
  • OpenAI — for AI video generation (no user data is sent to OpenAI)

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information during transmission and authentication.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke access at any time by disconnecting RingClip AI from your TikTok account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact
